FAQ
Questions
What happens after I purchase?+
We immediately provision your system — purchase domains, create mailboxes, configure DNS, and start warmup. You'll receive a dashboard login within minutes. Warmup takes ~14 days, then live outreach begins automatically.
Do I need to write any email copy?+
No. Our AI generates all email copy based on your service description and target market. It then A/B tests multiple variations and automatically promotes winners. The system gets smarter every week.
Where do the leads come from?+
We source leads matching your ideal customer profile — industry, company size, job title, location. Fresh contacts delivered directly into your campaigns. You never need to buy a lead list.
Why does warmup take 14 days?+
Email providers like Gmail and Outlook need to see that your mailboxes are legitimate before they'll deliver emails to the inbox. Skipping warmup means your emails land in spam. We build your reputation properly so you get 95%+ inbox placement.
What kind of results can I expect?+
At full capacity, clients typically see reply rates of 2-4% which translates to 40-80+ warm leads per month on the Growth plan. The self-learning AI improves rates over time — clients who've been running for 3+ months see significantly higher performance.
Can I connect my own systems via API?+
Yes. Every account includes API access. You can read campaign data, performance metrics, and lead information. Growth and Scale plans include write access to update copy and targeting, plus webhook notifications for real-time events.
Is there a contract or lock-in?+
No contracts. Cancel anytime. We recommend a minimum 3-month commitment to see the full benefit of the self-learning AI, but you're free to cancel whenever you want.
